What is all this about?

Synbio CATaLyST is an innovative startup dedicated to develop software tools focused on automating and accelerating processes, by performing simulations, analyzing data and providing customized services to research groups and biotech companies, thereby supporting the advancement of biosciences.

At Synbio CATaLyST, we understand the importance of having the right tools, adapting their needs to those of researchers, and providing the necessary support so that these researchers have the best available technologies, saving time and maximizing their results.

How this idea started?

Synbio CATaLyST was born as a promising idea by students at the Faculty of Sciences and the Higher Technical School of Computer Engineering at the University of Málaga. This was in response to the students' observations regarding the limited availability of quality software tools, the poor quality of certain graphical interfaces, and the researchers' need for easy-to-use software to analyze data quickly and consistently.

Synbio CATaLyST seeks to become a benchmark for technological solutions for all companies and research groups seeking to increase their performance in the biosciences field.
